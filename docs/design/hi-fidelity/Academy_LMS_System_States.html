Loading, Empty & Error

The states a screen is in before it has the data it wants — most of a real product's screen-time, and the part a happy-path mockup never shows. Every pattern below follows the same voice: explain what happened and what to do next, never apologize, never stay vague.

Skeletons

Shaped like the content they'll become, not a generic gray box — a table skeleton looks like table rows, a card skeleton looks like cards.

Inline & Form Errors

Specific about what's wrong and how to fix it — never just "Invalid input."

31 February isn't a valid date — check the day and month.
This document exceeds the 10MB limit. Compress it or upload a smaller scan.

Network / Retry

Transient failures — the interface says what it's doing about it, not just that something broke.

Couldn't save your last answer — retrying automatically…
Payment reconciliation (from the Applicant flow)
Reconciliation pending
Your bank shows this differently than our records. Our finance team is checking — no action needed yet.

403 / 404 / 500

Full-page failures — same voice as everything else, no stack traces, always a way back.

🔒

Your role doesn't include this page. If you think that's wrong, ask your administrator.
🔍

⚠

Our team's been notified. Your progress up to this point is saved.
